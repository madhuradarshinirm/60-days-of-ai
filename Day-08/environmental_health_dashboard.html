    Data & Methodology

    AQI readings reflect a live snapshot compiled from public air-quality dashboards on 11 July 2026. Where a station did not publish granular PM2.5/PM10 alongside AQI, those values were derived using standard CPCB AQI↔PM2.5 breakpoint interpolation and a regional PM10/PM2.5 ratio — flagged as estimated in the dataset. Water Quality Scores are a composite (0–100) built from the 2019 BIS drinking-water study (comparing 21 state capitals), state-level Water Quality Index figures (CPCB / NITI Aayog CWMI), and reported contamination incidents — used here for relative, directional comparison rather than a certified regulatory score. The Environmental Health Score weights Air 60% / Water 40%, reflecting the more immediate day-to-day health impact of air exposure. This dashboard is an analytical/educational tool, not medical or regulatory advice.
