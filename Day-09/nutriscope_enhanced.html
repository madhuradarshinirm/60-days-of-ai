Nutrition Sources & Methodology

  • Basal metabolic rate: Mifflin-St Jeor equation, scaled by activity level to estimate total daily energy expenditure (TDEE).
  • Protein targets: general activity-scaled intake guidance (1.0–1.7 g/kg body weight), toward the higher end for active individuals.
  • Micronutrient reference intakes: approximate adult RDAs adapted from ICMR-NIN Dietary Guidelines for Indians and general international RDA ranges (values vary by age, sex, and country).
  • Sodium is shown as an upper intake limit (~2300 mg/day), not a target to reach — lower percentages are better.
  • Food composition values: representative per-100g estimates compiled from Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT) and USDA FoodData Central. Actual values vary by brand, cooking method, and preparation.
Figures are approximations for educational exploration, not laboratory-verified nutrient analyses. For precise values, refer to product labels or official nutrition databases.
